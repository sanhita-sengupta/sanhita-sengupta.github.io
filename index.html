About

I am a data scientist and statistician working at the intersection of AI/ML, clinical trial analytics, and real-world evidence in the pharmaceutical industry. Currently serving as a Senior Manager at Bristol Myers Squibb, I develop innovative, end-to-end data solutions using multimodal clinical trial data and real-world data (RWD) to advance precision medicine.

I hold a Ph.D. in Statistics from the University of Minnesota and am currently pursuing a Master’s in Data Science at Monroe College. My academic and industry experiences span causal inference, hybrid control arm design, covariate adjustment, and predictive modeling in oncology and cell/gene therapies.

I am actively involved in the statistical community and currently serve as the Secretary of the American Statistical Association’s New Jersey Chapter. I have authored peer-reviewed publications, presented at international conferences (JSM, ASCO, IISA), and reviewed for top-tier journals in statistical methodology and biomedical research.
